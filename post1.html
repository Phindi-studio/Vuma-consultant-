Why Vuma Fibre May Not Be Working After Payment

If your Vuma Fibre is not working after payment, don’t panic. In most cases, this is part of the normal activation process in South Africa.

Most delays are caused by activation or system processing — not a problem with your fibre installation.

Common Reasons for This Delay

  • Activation in progress: Your order is still being processed in the system.
  • System update delay: The line has not fully synced yet.
  • Installation not fully completed: Setup may still be finalising.

How Long It Usually Takes

Most Vuma Fibre applications take between 24 to 48 hours to become fully active after payment or installation. Delays can happen depending on demand in your area.

What You Should Do

  • Restart your router and wait a few minutes
  • Check if installation was confirmed as complete
  • Allow up to 48 hours for activation

Important Note

If your fibre is still not working after 48 hours, it is best to get help so your application or activation can be checked properly.
