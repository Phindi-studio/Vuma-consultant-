Vuma Fibre Prepaid vs Recurring Explained

Choosing between prepaid and recurring Vuma Fibre depends on your budget and how you prefer to manage your internet payments.

Prepaid gives flexibility, while recurring gives lower monthly cost and consistency.

Prepaid Vuma Fibre (R437)

  • No monthly contract
  • You pay when you need internet
  • Good for flexibility and short-term use

Recurring Vuma Fibre (R417)

  • Lower monthly cost
  • Automatic monthly billing
  • Better for long-term usage

Simple Difference

Prepaid is flexible. Recurring is more stable and cost-effective over time.
