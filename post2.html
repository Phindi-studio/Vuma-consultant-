How Long Does Vuma Fibre Installation Take?

Vuma Fibre installation in South Africa usually takes a few days, depending on your area, demand, and technician availability.

Most installations take 24 to 72 hours after your application is approved and processed.

Average Installation Time

In most areas, installation takes between 1 to 3 days after your order has been confirmed.

What Can Delay Installation?

  • High demand in your area
  • Technician availability
  • Weather conditions
  • System processing delays

What Happens After You Apply?

  • Your order is processed and checked
  • Technician is assigned
  • Installation is scheduled
  • Fibre is connected and activated

Important Tip

Activation may take a few extra hours after installation. This is normal and part of the system process.
